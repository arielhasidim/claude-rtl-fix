Claude RTL Fix — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What this extension does

Claude RTL Fix is a Safari browser extension that detects Hebrew and Arabic text in responses on claude.ai and applies right-to-left (RTL) text direction automatically.

Data collection

We collect no data whatsoever. The extension:

  • Does not transmit any information to any server
  • Does not read, store, or log your conversations
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or cookies
  • Operates entirely within your browser, locally

Permissions

The extension requests access to claude.ai only, solely to apply CSS text-direction changes. No other sites are accessed.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.
