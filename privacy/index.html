Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Claude RTL Fix is a Safari extension that adjusts the text direction of Hebrew and Arabic content on claude.ai. Your privacy is simple to describe because the extension does almost nothing with data.

What we collect

Nothing. The extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or browsing data.

What it does

The extension reads the text already shown on claude.ai pages in your browser only to decide which paragraphs should be displayed right-to-left. This happens entirely on your device. No text is ever sent anywhere.

Permissions

The extension requests no special permissions and runs only on claude.ai. It has no access to other websites.

Third parties

There are no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party services of any kind.
